# SmartMirror for Barbershops
## Transform Your Shop with Smart Technology

---

## Overview

SmartMirror is a modern, interactive display system designed specifically for barbershops. It combines appointment management, customer recognition, and real-time information to create a premium experience for both barbers and customers.

---

## Key Features

### 1. Interactive Mirror Display
Your shop mirrors become smart information hubs showing:
- **Real-time clock and date**
- **Local weather forecast** (5-day outlook)
- **Today's appointments** at a glance
- **Breaking news feed** from trusted sources
- **Holiday calendar** integration
- **Custom messages** from shop management

### 2. Face Recognition
- **Personalized greetings** when customers approach the mirror
- **Privacy-focused** - no personal attributes displayed publicly
- **Customer self-registration** via QR code on their phone
- Works with existing shop cameras

### 3. Voice Commands
Customers and barbers can interact hands-free:
- "Mirror mirror, show appointments"
- "Mirror mirror, what's the weather?"
- "Mirror mirror, show messages"
- "Mirror mirror, what time is it?"

### 4. Telegram Bot Integration (@BarberMirrorBot)
Manage your shop from your phone:
- **Book appointments** for walk-ins or calls
- **Track daily earnings** and budget goals
- **Send messages** to the mirror display
- **View customer history** and preferences
- **Remote mirror control** from anywhere

### 5. Multi-Tenant Architecture
Perfect for shop owners with multiple locations:
- **Separate data** for each barbershop
- **Multiple mirrors** per location
- **Individual barber accounts**
- **Centralized management**

## Deployment Options

SmartMirror is cloud-hosted, so your display hardware just needs a web browser. Choose the option that fits your budget and needs.

### Recommended Hardware

| Option | Cost | Best For |
|--------|------|----------|
| **Android TV Box** | $40-60 | Most shops - affordable, reliable, easy setup |
| **Amazon Fire TV Stick** | $30-50 | Budget option - plug and play |
| **Orange Pi 5** | $60-90 | Tech-savvy owners wanting more control |
| **Mini PC / Intel NUC** | $150-300 | Premium installs, maximum performance |
| **Repurposed Laptop** | Free | Testing or budget-conscious shops |
| **Smart TV with Browser** | Varies | Use your existing TV |

### Kiosk Mode Setup

The display runs in "kiosk mode" - fullscreen, locked to SmartMirror, auto-restarts if needed.

**For Android/Fire TV:**
- Install "Fully Kiosk Browser" app
- Point to your SmartMirror URL
- Device stays locked to the mirror display

**For PC/Laptop:**
- Chrome opens in fullscreen kiosk mode
- Auto-starts when device powers on
- No desktop or other apps visible

### Audio Options

For voice greetings and responses:
- **Built-in speakers** - Use the TV or monitor's speakers
- **Bluetooth speaker** - Connect any Bluetooth speaker for better sound
- **Wired speakers** - USB or 3.5mm audio output

### What You Need

1. **Display** - Any TV or monitor (we recommend 32" or larger)
2. **Display device** - Android box, Fire Stick, or mini PC
3. **Internet** - WiFi or ethernet connection
4. **Camera** (optional) - USB webcam for face recognition
5. **Speaker** (optional) - For voice greetings
