# SmartMirror for Barbershops
## Transform Your Shop with Smart Technology

---

## Overview

SmartMirror is a modern, interactive display system designed specifically for barbershops. It combines appointment management, customer recognition, and real-time information to create a premium experience for both barbers and customers.

---

## Key Features

### 1. Interactive Mirror Display
Your shop mirrors become smart information hubs showing:
- **Real-time clock and date**
- **Local weather forecast** (5-day outlook)
- **Today's appointments** at a glance
- **Breaking news feed** from trusted sources
- **Holiday calendar** integration
- **Custom messages** from shop management

### 2. Face Recognition
- **Personalized greetings** when customers approach the mirror
- **Privacy-focused** - no personal attributes displayed publicly
- **Customer self-registration** via QR code on their phone
- Works with existing shop cameras

### 3. Voice Commands
Customers and barbers can interact hands-free:
- "Mirror mirror, show appointments"
- "Mirror mirror, what's the weather?"
- "Mirror mirror, show messages"
- "Mirror mirror, what time is it?"

### 4. Telegram Bot Integration (@BarberMirrorBot)
Manage your shop from your phone:
- **Book appointments** for walk-ins or calls
- **Track daily earnings** and budget goals
- **Send messages** to the mirror display
- **View customer history** and preferences
- **Remote mirror control** from anywhere

### 5. Multi-Tenant Architecture
Perfect for shop owners with multiple locations:
- **Separate data** for each barbershop
- **Multiple mirrors** per location
- **Individual barber accounts**
- **Centralized management**

## Role-Based Access

### Shop Admin
- Manage all barbers and their schedules
- Set shop-wide services and pricing
- Track total revenue and budget targets
- Configure mirrors and modules
- View all appointments across the shop

### Barbers
- View personal appointment schedule
- Track individual earnings
- Receive notifications for bookings
- Access customer preferences

## Appointment System

- **Telegram booking** - Customers message the bot to book
- **Walk-in queue** - Add customers on the spot
- **Barber selection** - Book with specific barbers or "Any available"
- **Service catalog** - Display services with pricing
- **Real-time updates** - Mirror shows today's schedule

- **Secure** - JWT authentication, encrypted data

- **Customizable** - Enable/disable modules per mirror
