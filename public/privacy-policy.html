Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 2024

1. Introduction

SmartMirror ("we," "our," or "us") provides a smart mirror platform for barbershops. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our services.

By using SmartMirror, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Personal Information: Name, phone number, email address when you create an account or book appointments
  • Appointment Data: Services booked, appointment times, barber preferences
  • Face Recognition Data: Facial descriptors for optional face recognition features (stored locally or with your consent)
  • Usage Data: How you interact with our platform
  • Communication Data: Messages sent through our platform, SMS notification preferences

3. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:

  • Provide and maintain our services
  • Send appointment reminders and confirmations via SMS
  • Notify you about walk-in queue status
  • Personalize your experience through face recognition (with consent)
  • Process transactions and manage appointments
  • Communicate with you about service updates
  • Improve our platform and services

4. SMS Communications

When you provide your phone number, you may receive:

  • Appointment reminder messages
  • Appointment confirmation messages
  • Walk-in queue notifications
  • Service-related updates

You can opt-out of SMS communications at any time by replying STOP to any message. Message and data rates may apply. Message frequency varies.

We do not share your phone number with third parties for marketing purposes.

5. Face Recognition Data

Our platform offers optional face recognition features:

  • Face recognition is opt-in and requires explicit consent
  • Facial descriptors are stored securely and used only for identification
  • You can request deletion of your face data at any time
  • We do not share face data with third parties
  • No personal attributes (age, gender, emotions) are stored or displayed

6. Data Sharing

We may share your information with:

  • Barbershops: The shop you book with receives your appointment and contact information
  • Service Providers: Third-party services that help us operate (e.g., SMS delivery through Twilio)
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law or to protect our rights

We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

7. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information, including:

  • Encrypted data transmission (HTTPS)
  • Secure database storage
  • Access controls and authentication
  • Regular security reviews

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal information
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Opt-out of SMS communications
  • Withdraw consent for face recognition

To exercise these rights, contact your barbershop or reach out through our platform.

9. Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as necessary to provide our services and comply with legal obligations. Appointment history may be retained for business records. You can request deletion of your data at any time.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.

11. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact your barbershop directly or reach out through our platform.
