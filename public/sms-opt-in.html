SmartMirror SMS Notifications

Stay connected with your barbershop appointments

What You're Signing Up For

By providing your phone number to SmartMirror or your barbershop, you consent to receive the following SMS notifications:

  • Appointment Reminders - We'll text you before your scheduled appointment so you don't forget
  • Appointment Confirmations - Receive confirmation when your appointment is booked
  • Walk-in Queue Updates - If you join a walk-in queue, we'll notify you when it's almost your turn
  • Service Updates - Important updates about your scheduled services

How to Opt-In

You can opt-in to receive SMS notifications in the following ways:

  • Providing your phone number when booking an appointment online
  • Giving your phone number to your barber or the front desk
  • Adding your phone number in the customer registration form
  • Texting "START" to our business number
Note: Message and data rates may apply. Message frequency varies based on your appointment schedule.

How to Manage Your Preferences

You have full control over your SMS notifications:

STOP - Unsubscribe HELP - Get assistance CONFIRM - Confirm appointment CANCEL - Cancel appointment

To stop receiving messages at any time, simply reply STOP to any message you receive from us. You will receive a confirmation that you've been unsubscribed.

For help, reply HELP or contact your barbershop directly.

Your Privacy

We respect your privacy and will never:

  • Share your phone number with third parties for marketing
  • Send promotional or advertising messages without consent
  • Sell your contact information

For more details, see our Privacy Policy and Terms of Service.

Contact Information

If you have questions about SMS notifications, please contact your barbershop directly or reach out through our platform.

SmartMirror is a service platform for barbershops. Individual shops manage their own customer communications.
